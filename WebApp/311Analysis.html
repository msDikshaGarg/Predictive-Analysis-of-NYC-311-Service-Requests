Analysis of NYC 311 Service Requests

About the Project

About

We offer our services in making the NYC 311 Service Request data easier to interpret. We do this by making the analysis of data fun to understand with our interactive analysis depiction.

This data visualization is a dashboard allowing users to check the approximate resolution time to their request. This also shows the past analysis of the data with respect to different parameters. To know your approximate resolution time, click the button in the prediction section. First choose your zip code from the dropdown. Furthermore, you can choose the type of complaint and day of the week. Hit submit to know your predicted response time.

Our analysis can also be used to gauge an area before acquiring an apartment at that location and various other such purposes.
